Our Story

Abakaliki's Most Trusted Pig Farm

Oluka Farms is a locally rooted, commercially driven pig farm operating from 13 Akam Street, Ntezi Abba, Abakaliki, Ebonyi State. We have built a solid reputation for delivering healthy, well-fed pigs to families, restaurants, and business buyers across the region.

Our farm maintains strict hygiene standards, quality feed protocols, and responsible breeding practices — all designed to give you livestock that is strong, healthy, and market-ready. Whether you need a single pig for a ceremony or bulk supply for your business, Oluka Farms is the name Abakaliki trusts.
